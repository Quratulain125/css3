Demonstrating Positions

This section is here to create enough content to make the page scrollable.As you scroll down ,notice a few things: The header at the very top has position:sticky.It scrolls with the content until it hits the top of viewport,where it "sticks" in place. The profile card above has position:fixed,which means it stays in the same place on the screen even as you scroll. The footer at the bottom has position:absolute,which means it is positioned relative to the nearest positioned ancestor (in this case, the body element).
